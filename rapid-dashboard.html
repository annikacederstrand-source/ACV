NEPA Review — Required at Multiple Phases

National Environmental Policy Act review is triggered at leasing, drilling (each GDP), and utilization. NEPA timelines range 1–4 years depending on review type. GDPs are not eligible for Categorical Exclusion (CX) — expect EA or DNA.
